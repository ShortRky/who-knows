# UNIQLO-Inspired E-commerce Design

## Design Philosophy

### Color Palette
- **Primary**: Clean white (#FFFFFF) - representing purity and simplicity
- **Secondary**: Deep charcoal (#2D2D2D) - for text and accents
- **Accent**: Warm red (#DC143C) - for call-to-action buttons and highlights
- **Supporting**: Light gray (#F8F8F8) - for backgrounds and subtle divisions

### Typography
- **Display Font**: "Helvetica Neue" - Bold, modern sans-serif for headings
- **Body Font**: "Helvetica Neue" - Clean, readable for product descriptions and content
- **UI Font**: "Helvetica Neue" - Consistent across all interface elements

### Visual Language
- Minimalist approach with generous white space
- Grid-based layouts for product organization
- High-quality product photography against clean backgrounds
- Subtle hover effects and smooth transitions
- Clean geometric shapes and precise alignment

## Visual Effects

### Used Libraries
- **Anime.js**: For smooth product card animations and page transitions
- **Splide.js**: For hero image carousel and product image galleries
- **ECharts.js**: For sales analytics and product popularity visualization
- **Typed.js**: For dynamic text effects in hero section
- **p5.js**: For creative background patterns and visual elements

### Animation & Styling
- **Product Cards**: Subtle scale and shadow effects on hover
- **Navigation**: Smooth underline animations for active states
- **Loading States**: Elegant skeleton loading for product grids
- **Scroll Effects**: Gentle fade-in animations as content enters viewport
- **Cart Interactions**: Smooth quantity adjustments and item removals

### Header Effect
- Clean navigation bar with subtle shadow
- Sticky positioning with smooth background transition
- Shopping cart icon with animated item count badge
- Search bar with expanding animation on focus

### Background Treatment
- Consistent white background throughout
- Subtle geometric patterns using p5.js for visual interest
- No gradients - maintaining clean, flat design aesthetic
- Product sections differentiated by light gray backgrounds
